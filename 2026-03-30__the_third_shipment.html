The hours pass slowly on the Old Svalich Road. At first, the journey is merely long, as the road stretches on in that gray, Barovian way. Conversation comes and goes. Even the driver's occasional muttering gives way to silence as the hours pass. Then, gradually, the road begins to rise, and the passes through the hills begin to narrow. Then snow begins. Light at first, with a scattering of flakes, but as the road climbs further, it intensifies and accumulates on your armor and cloaks, and on the road. Before long, the road is no longer brown and gray, but white. Each turn of the wagon wheel takes effort now, as snow gathers in drifts, and the once-clear road becomes an uncertain track through the pass. It's peaceful though, as the sound is dulled by the blanket of snow. Though, it doesn’t take much imagination to see it... How something could watch from above. Hide just beyond the tree line... How easily a wagon could be stopped here… with nowhere to turn, nowhere to run.

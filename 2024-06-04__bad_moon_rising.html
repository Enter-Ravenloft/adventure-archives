# BAD MOON RISING
DM khaotye BOT 06-06-2024 22:21
It is an early spring evening in Barovia. A chill still lingers in the air, the ever present mist stifling the seasonal warmings. It has been rainy for the past several days, the ground soft underfoot. Having been contracted by citizens of Krezk, the five of you have gathered here, prepared to go on a hunt. Following their directions to where the slaughterings have taken place, you find yourselves deep in the Svalich Woods as night falls. The moon is full in all of her glory tonight, her luminescence lighting the forest better than most nights. Following a trail through the woods you hear howls in the distance, wolves serenading the moon with songs of violence. You see the occasional set of footprints, large and clawed, obviously from a large canid. Nearby, the sound of branches snapping breaks the silence in the air, echoing throughout the misty forest. And wolves continue to sing - much, much closer.
Torboro (nick) BOT 06-06-2024 22:32
Krezk... Torboro hadn't been in this neck of the woods since the blood moon. But a job was a job, and he'd had a little bit of interest in seeing more of what Barovia had to offer. There was so much he had to presume was long-explored but maybe long-forgotten. It was to this end he decided to take this job, more to sate his curiosity than anything else. Besides, he'd always liked wolves. Maybe he'd be able to find a pup out here. And he needed to get away from Vallaki for a while... After his last adventure, he'd found out about his close friend's passing. It was at the funeral, when he'd been in a very contemplative headspace, that he'd come to terms with what he understood to be his curse... And now, he had to find something to cure this ailment, because right now, Torboro was starving to death. (edited)

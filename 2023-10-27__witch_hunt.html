Wiz-DM BOT 29-10-2023 15:31
Nightmares are common in these lands. Sleep provides little sanctuary from the horrors of Barovia. Even sleepless elves and constructs are susceptible to visions of horror during trances. Such is the way of the land. It was this past night such horrors struck. Dreams of bloody blades, and freezing blood. The gnawing stings of insects. A murder of crows. A dragon's roar. These unsettling visions troubled many who rested during the night. Though only six were attuned enough to sense the deeper calling that came with these visions. These six were drawn to a lone hut in the wilds of Barovia. Past the fringes of what passed as civilized here. The hut was familiar to some, to others a novelty. Within resided the rumored hag of the wilds. A powerful seer that exchanges boons for memory. What the seer did with these memories was unknown. As was her goal in these lands. Nevertheless the six drawn here could not help but feel an unsettling sense of foreboding as they approached the lone hut before them.

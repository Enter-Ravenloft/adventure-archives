Dungeon Master (disco) BOT 11-03-2025 01:01
From the shores of Lake Zarovich, a strange sight has appeared once, twice, three days in a row. At the third hour past midday, for a few short minutes, a craggy island in the distance is bathed in sunlight. Naturally, such a thing is unheard of in the ever-enclouded bleakness of Barovia. The seasoned adventurers know that this must certainly be a trap, but those recent to these lands are surely prone to it. To taste sunlight once more is worth braving any danger! Some adventurers had already departed by boat, and returned to tell tales of beasts, hags, and demons all connected somehow to the light, but no real answers. It is another day, and the light may yet return, and you even have some of that first group among you! So you've chartered a boat much like before, but you are wiser now: you bring some off-duty Vallaki guards with you, and you set sail earlier in the day, so as to arrive ahead of the light. That way you can handle any immediate threats, and then bask when it does finally dawn on you, so to speak. You are on deck, and so far the wind has been favorable. Except that the mate in the crow's nest calls down, and his voice is filled with fear. "Ach, an attack! Prepare yerselves!!" There is a storm, but it is not on the horizon. It is upon you, with barely any warning!

Symphony of Suffering - Start Ghost music, some have taken to calling it. To some, it is nothing but an urban legend. Mysterious music, wafting from the forest, luring travellers to an untimely demise. To others, it is a very real truth, driving some so far as to block their ears with wax and cotton when travelling this particular stretch of woods. The origins of the music, of course, are as varied as the people speaking of it in hushed barroom whispers. Siren song, trickster fae, banshee wails, spells of enchantment, demonic pipers, and some even go so far as to say it's just someone really good at playing music, who has chosen to do so deep in the Barovian Woods, for reasons known only to themselves. Whatever the truth of the matter, one thing is clear. The mysterious music is very real. Even now, as you stand in the chill mists that waft through the air, you can hear the distant melodies being played out by an unseen orchestra. Simply hearing it here, you feel no compulsion at present, but clearly something has happened here. The carriage more than confirms that. Just off to the side of the road, a simple carriage sits, horses tied to the front shifting and whinnying nervously. Of the driver, and the occupants, there is no sign. No obvious damage, no signs of a physical altercation, and no bodies, it's as if they pulled over, left the vehicle, and wandered off into the woods. The fact that the carriage was due to reach Vallaki two days ago, and its failure to do so is what prompted the most recent request for daring adventurers to investigate, makes you think this probably isn't some simple pit stop.

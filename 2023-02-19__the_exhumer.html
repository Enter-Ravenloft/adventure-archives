There is the faint sound of clicking and rhythmic grinding and as the group push into the open space before them, they can see a small foot bridge that spans a deep crevasse, the other side home to the almost 50ft tower of stacked, bleached bones. Standing silently beside it, two skeletons adorned in metallic armor look across the clearing, small purple pin pricks of light flicker deep within their eye sockets...

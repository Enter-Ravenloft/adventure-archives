# Barny Trouble
Dungeon Master (disco) BOT 31-03-2024 13:38
Midday. Vallaki. The Blue Water Inn. The door of the inn swings open, and a human stumbles in. He is as common as a Barovian citizen gets--dirty, disheartened, scared, unremarkable. His sunken eyes scan the room, looking for someone, anyone. He sees almost exclusively elves among the patrons, and looks only more scared. For some few moments he stands there awkwardly, dancing from foot. His mouth opens and closes a few times as he builds--or ebbs--his resolve. Finally he croaks out his request to the common room at large, whilst avoiding eye contact with the patrons themselves. "G-gold. I offer g-good gold f-for a service. S-something in m-my barn, something unnatural. Gold for anyone who cleans it out." The words are spoken like hot air fleeing a balloon, and the man looks thoroughly deflated. He collapses into an unoccupied chair and buries his hands in his thinning hair, not daring to look up.

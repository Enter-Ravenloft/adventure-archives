Act I - A Call for Help

The road east of Vallaki is mostly quiet. The trees of the Svalich Woods - the Wilds as you know them - loom dark to the north as the sun begins to set. Not far from the Miller farmstead, a grim sight waits at the roadside: a goat lies sprawled in the dirt, its body stiff, crawling with flies. What draws the eye is the hide - or what remains of it. The creature’s fur and hide is ripped to shreds and torn away almost completely. A few villagers stand nearby, muttering and making superstitious gestures, glance nervously up the road, as you move toward them. And when the first of you appears, armed and travel-worn, their chatter dies. Without a word, they scatter, hurrying back toward Vallaki rather than risk being caught in whatever trouble follows visiting adventurers. The only sounds left are the lazy buzzing of flies and the creak and rustle of the farmhouse’s weathered scarecrows mounted on large branches in front of the fields. Whatever they are meant to scare, its not enough to ward off whatever caused this frightful scene. The Miller farmstead stands just beyond the field, it's bright red door either an invitation or warning - it's not clear which. This is the place the that the job notice described, and the scene certainly matches the call for help - but you don't need skills or experience in natural or medical sciences to know that this is not the work of wolves or raiders. Your only questions now are, who would do this, and who else is brave enough to join you in this investigation?

## Shopping Spree (part 2) Yesterday had been an odd day in Vallaki, as the general store, Dusklight Supply, had been closed all day. Yet, despite being closed, four adventurers had been seen entering through the back door. Moreover, while they were inside, Bethel had been seen leaving! Rumors about, though no one has seen Bethel since. Had she returned? What the denizens of Vallaki do not yet know are the wild happenings of the day inside of the shop. First, Bethel closed it because she noticed that Dusklight was getting burgled, every day. Second, a locket was taken off of her neck while she slept. Third, the thieves weren't triggering Guardian, the flesh golem who had attached herself to Bethel's service. The adventurers sleuthed about, and identified first that Redcaps--a rather bellicose type of feyfolk--had dug a tunnel into the storage cellar. However, engrossed in their investigation, they lost sight of Bethel--the real Bethel--and unknowingly met her doppelganger. After a hard fight in the cellar, they jammed up the tunnel and found the real Bethel tied up in the attic! The doppelganger had managed to escape, promising to fetch guards. Now a night has gone by, with the adventurers camping out in the store, but no guards have arrived. The tunnel has yet to be explored, and the doppelganger is in the wind. Bethel--the real one--still needs help.

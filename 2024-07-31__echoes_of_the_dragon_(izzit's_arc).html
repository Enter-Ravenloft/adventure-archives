Izzit dreams of a majestic silver dragon, its scales gleaming with an otherworldly light, flying over a dark, desolate landscape. The dragon's mournful roar echoes through the night, carrying a sense of profound loss and unfulfilled duty. She sees flashes of a grand, crumbling mansion, a spectral knight bowing in sorrow. Each time she awakens, the images linger, accompanied by a feeling of urgency and a whisper in her mind, "Come to Argynvostholt." The dream repeats night after night, no mere coincidence but a call for her to uncover the truth and restore what was lost.

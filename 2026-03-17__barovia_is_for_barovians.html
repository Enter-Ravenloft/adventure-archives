Disappearances in Barovia -- well, sadly, that's just the norm. You might have a lovely dinner with a friend one evening, and then never hear from the again. No eulogy, no funeral, no headstone to mark their body. Over time, you might even question yourself: did this friend ever exist, or was it only a figment of my imagination? *Recently, there's been some disturbing rumors. Adventurers who have built up small reputations for helping locals out with wolves, undead, or monsters who ruin farmsteads have disappeared mid-contract, leaving no word as to what happened to them despite past records of good communication. New arrivals who have been brought in by the hungry mists have been found dead at the edge of the woods before they even make it to town -- their bodies showing telltale signs of being harmed by weapons or magic, not claws or teeth belonging to beasts. Threatening notes have been pinned to the doors of inn rooms being rented out by adventurers using daggers and knives, and the anonymous notes would say:* "Get out. Leave. You are not wanted here." But rumors are rumors, and you have plenty to worry about as a survivor here, until today. You are sitting in the dining room of Vallaki's Blue Water Inn at midday when the tavern door bursts open, and a young woman, face streaming with tears, rushes in crying for help. (edited)

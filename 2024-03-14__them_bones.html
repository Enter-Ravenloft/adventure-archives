Wiz-DM BOT 14-03-2024 23:09
The first sign that something was wrong was the increase of grave robberies in Barovia's cemetery. Graves long forgotten were haphazardly excavated, not by shovel, but by claw. The disappearance of wildlife was another sign. The owls mice and even wolves in the surrounding woods vanished seemingly overnight. Increased sightings of walking skeletons pushed Burgomaster Ismark to call for an investigation. Hiring some of Barovia's more formidable monster slayers, the good Burgomaster sent a group of four to investigate the cause of these unusual events. The group finds a walking skeleton easily enough. An amalgamation of bones from several creatures. The head of a pig, hooves like a deer and femur's for arms, giving it an unnatural reach. Currently those arms carries the corpse of a fawn, its throat torn out. It takes the corpse to a large pile of bones, teaming with animated skeletons. These bones busy themselves with adding to the ivory pile before them.

## Colosseum "I promise ya, there's good coin ta be made!" That may have been what the man had actually said, or the woman had whispered, or the child had sung, or if those words were merely the echoes that Moondrop, Tavoros, and Red could remember. For one reason or another, the prospect of following their individual guides overwhelmed their sense of caution. Curiosity, pride, greed, boredom. Only in Scrihk's case did the 'Colosseum' not hold any of its own allure, yet he too followed. What other tidbits of information had the guides provided? That Razzle Dazzle wasn't involved, that the payout would be grander than what they had seen on the arena sands, and that the audience would be a much livelier crowd. Or perhaps those things had been hinted at, but not said outright. These people spoke in hints and circles, promising the whole world and nothing at the same time. When finally the various journeys end, after winding through roads and woods and marshes, the would-be gladiators find themselves on the lip of the north edge of a quarry. A wooden platform, with two men working a heavy winch, lowers the group down--though their guides remain above. A young woman with a scarred face greets them. "Well then, you've tired of Razzle pulling your tails," she winks at Moondrop, "and come to my wee colosseum then, have you? Oh I know, it does not look the part, yet. But with the money the fights have already been bringing in? We'll have this dug out in no time! Now you just head on in, and I'll announce your first fight!" As she speaks, the edges of the quarry quickly fill up with an audience. People clamor and chat amongst themselves as they find convenient spots to stand. This crowd seems much livelier than what one might expect of native Barovians.

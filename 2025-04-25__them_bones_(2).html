Wiz-DM BOT 26-04-2025 14:54
Four adventures stand around a forest clearing north west of Vallaki staring at the surreal scene. A pile of bones, at least 15' high, stands in the center of the clearing. About half a dozen animated skeletons surround the pile. Each carrying femurs, skulls and clavicles to add to the growing pile. Some of the working skeletons resemble the humanoid skeletal system, as if their flesh was melted away. Other skeletons are more of an amalgamation bones from many creatures, their bodies twisted in unnatural ways. Not all the bones added to the collection came from the long dead, as attributed to the dark red forming pools of blood below the pile. This would explain the lack of wildlife the group encountered coming to this clearing. As the group stars in horror a new animation separates from the pile. A large distorted beast with seven legs and three skulls. It lands onto the forest floor with a thunderous rattle and begins to march away, no doubt intent on finding its own additions to the pile. High atop the pile stands a humanoid skeleton watching the progress. Red glowing runes are carved into each of its bones. If it is directing the proceedings it does so wordlessly as it stands in a silent watch. The skeletons either have not noticed the party or are ignoring them for now as they shamble past, looking for more bones to add. The party mush act soon, or these undead creatures will find Valaki ripe for harvest.
